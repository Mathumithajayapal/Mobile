Oil & Lubrication

Engine oil, changes & filter maintenance

For most modern vehicles, we recommend an oil change every 5,000 to 7,500 miles, or every 6 months, whichever comes first. Full synthetic oil can last up to 10,000 miles. Always check your owner's manual for exact intervals.

Synthetic oil offers superior protection, better cold-weather flow, and longer change intervals. Conventional oil works well for older engines or low-mileage drivers. Our technicians help you choose based on your vehicle's specific needs.

The oil filter traps harmful contaminants like dust, metal particles, and sludge. Changing it with every oil change ensures clean oil circulation, maximizes engine protection, and extends your engine's lifespan.

Fluids & Vehicle Care

Coolant, transmission, brakes & more

We recommend checking transmission fluid every 30,000 miles or as specified by your manufacturer. Low or dirty fluid can cause shifting issues and potential transmission damage.

Typically every 30,000 to 50,000 miles. Regular coolant flushes prevent engine overheating, rust, and corrosion, keeping your cooling system efficient.

Brake fluid absorbs moisture over time, which reduces boiling point and braking performance. Replace every 2 years or 24,000 miles for optimal safety and responsiveness.
